About Our Business

Fresh, Hot Mini Donuts Cooked Live

We help Irish events create atmosphere, footfall, and a memorable food experience.

What We Do

LL American Donuts is a mobile mini donut business. We bring a professional donut machine and cooker setup to events such as markets, festivals, community days, and corporate gatherings.

Our Goal is Simple

Serve hot mini donuts quickly, keep the queue moving, and add atmosphere to your event.

Made Live On Site

Cooking on site creates that instant smell and excitement that pulls people in. It is a simple way to increase engagement around your vendor area.

Easy For Organisers

We are set up to be straightforward to host. We arrive prepared, operate professionally, and focus on quick service and customer experience.

Great Fit For Crowds

Our Mini donuts are a crowd pleaser. They work well for families, couples, and groups, making them ideal for busy public events.

Where We Operate

We are available for events across Ireland. If you are organising a market, festival, fundraiser, or corporate event, get in touch and we will confirm availability and requirements.
